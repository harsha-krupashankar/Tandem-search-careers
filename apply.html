Our esteemed boutique consulting firm is seeking an experienced Manager with a strong background in commercial due diligence and corporate strategy. As a Manager, you will play a pivotal role in leading and managing commercial due diligence engagements, providing strategic insights, and contributing to the growth of our firm. Previous experience in a Tier 1, Tier 2, consulting boutique, or MBB firm is required. The position is based in London.

Responsibilities:

  1. Lead and manage commercial due diligence engagements, taking total ownership of client projects from start to end, including project scoping, team management, and deliverable creation.

  2. Conduct comprehensive commercial due diligence assessments, evaluating target companies' financial performance, market dynamics, competitive positioning, and growth strategies.

  3. Apply strong corporate strategy skills to identify value creation opportunities, revenue/growth strategies, and synergies for potential mergers and acquisitions.

  4. Manage and coach direct reports, providing guidance, support, and mentorship to develop their consulting skills and enhance their professional growth.

  5. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including legal, finance, and operations experts, to ensure comprehensive due diligence coverage and facilitate seamless execution of engagements.

  6. Contribute to wider practice improvement initiatives, such as business development opportunities, team growth, and practice development.

     

Qualifications:

  1. Bachelor's degree in business, finance, or a related field. Advanced degree (MBA, MSc, or equivalent) is preferred.

  2. Previous experience in a Tier 1, Tier 2, consulting boutique, or MBB firm is required.

  3. Strong commercial due diligence experience, with a focus on deals-related projects, as well as corporate strategy skills.

  4. Proven experience in managing and coaching direct reports, fostering their professional growth and ensuring the delivery of high-quality work.

  5. Significant experience in conducting and managing commercial due diligence and vendor due diligence engagements, from project scoping to final deliverables.

  6. Strong M&A and revenue/growth strategy experience, with the ability to identify value creation opportunities for clients.

  7. Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to synthesize complex information and develop data-driven recommendations.

  8. Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to effectively convey complex ideas and recommendations to diverse stakeholders, including senior executives.

  9. London-based, with the ability to work on short and sharp projects.
